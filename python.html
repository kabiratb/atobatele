EV(electric vehicle) Adoption Network Capacity Planning

This project focuses on analyzing PowerCharge Utilities' challenges stemming from the growing adoption of electric vehicles. Key issues include heightened electricity demand during peak charging periods, which strains distribution networks. This situation leads to grid overloads, causing voltage fluctuations and localized outages, thus impacting overall grid reliability. Maintaining satisfactory and reliable charging services is essential for meeting EV owners' expectations and ensuring their loyalty. Balancing these demands while managing costs presents a significant challenge, requiring PowerCharge Utilities to optimize network investments effectively. The project aims to assess the distribution network's current capacity to handle increased EV charging loads, identifying potential vulnerabilities and implementing a data-driven strategy for network upgrades to enhance efficiency, minimize costs, and ensure reliable grid operation.
