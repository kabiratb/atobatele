Remittance and Economic patterns

The project seeks to develop and deploy a model that will predict the GDP and unemployment-rate of a country based on its remittance flow pattern. The following objectives will aid in achieving the desired aim:

  • To identify underlying trends in the remittance pattern for different countries over time.
  • To test common hypothesis on the effect of remittance flow on economic development KPIs such as GDP and unemployment rate, and evaluate the correlations among them.
  • To build a machine learning model to accurately predict the impact of remittance patterns on a country’s economic development.
  • To deploy the built machine learning model to interact with real-world data on remittance patterns and different country’s GDPs.
