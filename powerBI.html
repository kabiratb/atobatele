CREDIT CARD FRAUD

In this project i cleaned and analyzed a credit card fraud transaction, Fraudulent transactions are causing significant financial losses, with nearly half a million pounds stolen across 7 major banks in UK. While banks invest heavily in anti-fraud measures, they face a delicate balancing act—implementing robust fraud prevention without tampering the customer experience. Overly strict systems can lead to "false positives," disrupting legitimate transactions and frustrating cardholders. This report explores credit card fraud within the 7 major banks, aiming to uncover insights that can help strike the right balance between security and customer convenience. By examining this data, the goal is to offer banks actionable strategies to combat fraud effectively while ensuring a smooth experience for their customers.
