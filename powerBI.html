COVID-19 IMPACT ON AIRPORT TRAFFIC

This project analyzed the impact of COVID-19 on airport traffic, revealing significant declines in global travel, particularly in international and major city hubs, with domestic and regional markets showing greater resilience. Key insights include the USA and Canada leading recovery efforts, while stricter restrictions in countries like Chile and Australia slowed their rebound. Seasonal trends were disrupted, with sharp declines during peak travel periods. Recommendations include focusing on regional travel, enhancing safety measures, leveraging data for recovery, targeted marketing, and stakeholder collaboration. These strategies aim to rebuild traveler confidence and ensure long-term success in a post-pandemic world.
