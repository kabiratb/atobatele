NYC TAXI TRIP ANALYSIS

In this project i cleaned and analyzed a Nyc taxi trip data collated for 4 years with about 25million plus data. For this project i aim to answer some taxi trip data question and stick to trips that were not sent via "store and forward" to know if the trip is held in the vehicle memory.
1. To determine street hailed trip paid by card or cash with standard rate from 2017 to 2020.
2. What is the average trip we can expect this week.
3. What is the average fare per trip we expect to collect?
4. Which day of the week will be the busiest?
5. What will likely be the most pickup and drop off location?
There are 4 set of dataset for this analysis one each for the year from 2017 to 2020. Firstly I extracted the data to power query where I cleaned the data, merge and append the data for easy access, extract the particular year for report while i proceed to visualize.

Bitcoin Historical Analysis

This project shows an analysis of bitcoin data at 1min intervals from selected exchange for the time period of Jan 2012 to December March 2021, with a minute to minute updates of OHLC (Open, High, Low, Close), Volume in BTC and indicated currency, and weighted bitcoin price. Timestamps are in Unix time. Timestamps without any trades or activity have their data fields filled with NaNs. I extract the dataset to power query cleaned it, convert Unix time to real time. I then proceed to visualize.
