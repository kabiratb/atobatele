In this SQL data analysis project, I was tasked with extracting and analyzing relevant data to provide insights into the business performance of a company over time. The dataset provided was structured in a SQL file, containing information about products, orders, and sales transactions, I Utilize SQL queries to extract and transform the dataset to meet analysis requirements. Initially, I refined the dataset by filtering out irrelevant information for each analysis. Subsequently, I employed conditional logic to compute revenue, accommodating various discount scenarios and default prices.
